

44 N.D. 248
Supreme Court of North Dakota.
CRAWSON
v.
MIDDLEWEST GRAIN CO. et al.
June 21, 1919.

Syllabus by the Court.
This case is governed by the decision in Kluver v. Middlewest Grain Co., 39 N. D. ---, 173 N. W. 468.
 
Synopsis
Appeal from District Court, Ward County; Leighton, Judge.
 
Action by Charles Crawson against the Middlewest Grain Company and H. T. Hogy. From an order setting aside service of summons, plaintiff appeals. Order affirmed.
 
Attorneys and Law Firms
*475 W. H. Sibbald, of Minot, for appellant.
Fisk & Murphy, of Minot, for respondents.
Opinion

PER CURIAM.

This case was submitted together with Kluver v. Middlewest Grain Co., 39 N. D. ---, 173 N. W. 468. The facts in the instant case are identical with those in the Kluver Case, and on the authority of the decision rendered in that case the order appealed from is affirmed.
GRACE, J., concurs in the result.
